# FAQ

**What is this game?**
A tap-to-earn clicker game that runs as a Telegram Mini App. You tap to farm in-game coins, climb leagues and complete tasks.

**Do I need to install anything?**
No. It runs inside Telegram as a Mini App, or in the browser at [wm-mariupol.com](https://wm-mariupol.com).

**How do I earn coins?**
By tapping the coin and by completing daily tasks.

**What are leagues?**
League tiers represent your progression — the more you play and farm, the higher you climb.

**Can I play with friends?**
Yes — join or create a clan, and invite friends with your invite link.

**Does it use the TON blockchain?**
Yes — the game integrates with the TON blockchain and TON Connect wallets.

**Is there a referral program?**
Yes — share your invite code and earn rewards when friends join.
