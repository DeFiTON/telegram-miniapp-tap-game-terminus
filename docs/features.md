# Features

## Tap-to-earn gameplay

The core of the game: tap the coin to farm in-game coins. Simple, instant, addictive.

## Leagues and levels

Progress through league tiers and coin-progress levels as your balance grows.

## Daily tasks

Complete daily tasks for extra rewards and to keep your progress moving.

## Clans

Join or create a clan to play alongside other users.

## Referral / invite system

Every player gets an invite code. Invite friends and earn referral rewards when they join.

## Premium

Optional premium features are available in the game.

## TON blockchain integration

The game integrates with the TON blockchain and TON Connect wallets for its on-chain elements.

## Runs inside Telegram

The whole experience is a Telegram Mini App — there is nothing to install. Open it and play.
