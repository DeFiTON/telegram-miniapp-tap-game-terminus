# How to Play

This is a tap-to-earn clicker game that runs as a Telegram Mini App.

## Start playing

1. Open the game inside Telegram, or visit [wm-mariupol.com](https://wm-mariupol.com).
2. The game loads instantly — no installation required.

## Core loop

1. **Tap** the coin to farm in-game coins.
2. Your balance grows with every tap.
3. As you accumulate coins, you advance through **coin-progress levels**.

## Leagues

As your progress grows, you move up through **league tiers**. Higher leagues represent more experienced players.

## Daily tasks

Open the tasks section and complete **daily tasks** for bonus rewards.

## Clans

Join an existing **clan** or create your own to play together with other users.

## Invite friends

Share your **invite link** (with your invite code). Friends who join through your link contribute to your referral rewards.

## Wallet

Connect a TON Connect compatible wallet to use the game's TON-integrated features.
